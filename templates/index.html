Petrol Price Forcaster is a tool Created by Meet Chaklasiya, Final Year student at Pandit Deendayal Energy University. The user just have to enter the Date, Month and Year the model will automatically Predict the price, model is created with Machine algorithm and then connected with Flask for deployment on the website.
